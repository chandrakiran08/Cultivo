Rice

 

Telangana is in the rice bowl of South India, and RICE is cultivated in about 44 lakh acres. In the recent past, climatic changes have greatly influenced the amount rainfall, due to which the area cultivated with RICE has decreased.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Maize

 

Maize (Corn) is the second major cultivated crop in the state of Telangana in around 14 lakh acres producing annually 16 Lakh tonnes. It is used for human food and animal feed; it is also now widely used in corn starch industry, baby corn production etc.
